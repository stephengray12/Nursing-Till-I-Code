About Nursing 'Till I Code

Nursing 'Till I Code is a passion project created by a nurse-turned-developer to support fellow nursing students through accessible, free, and open-source educational tools.

The goal is simple: make it easier to succeed in nursing school without having to pay for basic learning resources. Whether it’s terminology quizzes, dosage calculators, or blog-style study guides, this platform is built to empower you on your journey.

Why This Exists

Nursing school is tough. Long clinical hours, demanding tests, and the constant need to remember everything. I built this site to blend my two worlds: nursing knowledge and coding skills.

This website will always remain free — because nurses deserve support, not paywalls.

What You'll Find Here

  • ✅ Helpful quizzes on nursing terminology and dosage
  • ✅ A growing blog with tips, study tools, and motivation
  • ✅ Open-source code — so you can contribute or learn from it
  • ✅ A contact form for suggestions and feedback
